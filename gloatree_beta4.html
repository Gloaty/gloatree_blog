Gaming Achievements

Call of Duty Zombies

WARNING: GAME SPOILERS AHEAD! 

In Call of Duty Zombies, especially Cold War, I have completed the Easter Eggs for Die Maschine and Forsaken. I thought that the end of the Forsaken Easter Egg was incredibly bittersweet.

The ending with every major hand at requiem being arrested, I thoght was incredibly bitter, but with the Forsaken being captured and taken away, that part was quite sweet, as it was a victory for requiem, before the director decides to rain on their parade. Also, the part where the director's face is revealed to be someone that is, or at least looks like Edward Richtofen was mindblowing. So yeah, a bit mindblowing, and quite bittersweet.

There isn't much to say about the Die machine Easter Egg, since it is a bit cut and dry, the particle accelerator is destroyed, and some of the outbreak zones have closed. The only sad parts about is Nacht der Untoten's destruction in the explosion, which means that there probably won't be another Nacht der Untoten map, apart from remasters of maps where it appears.

I think the developers making the Easter Eggs easier is okay. It allows those that are new to the series to complete their first quest, even me. My first quest was Die Maschine's quest. I have seen many classic zombies people dislike this change, but it's their opinion, and I can't change it, and I respect their opinions.

I have also hit some decent high rounds in Die Maschine and Mauer der Toten. I think that my highest round on Die Maschine is aroung 45, and Mauer der Toten is around the same as Die Maschine.
